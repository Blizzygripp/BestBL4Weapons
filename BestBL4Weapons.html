Essential Gear & Items

Daedalus / Energy Shield

Super Soldier

The gold standard for mobility. While the shield is full, gain +25% Fire Rate, +25% Movement Speed, and 1 Ammo/sec regen.

Drop: Vile Lictor (Umbral Foundry, Terminus Range)

Torgue / Armor Shield

Firewerks

On kill or Armor Segment break, spawns a cluster of homing missiles that hunt down nearby enemies. Elite for mobbing.

Drop: Splashzone (Riptide Grotto, Fadefields)

Torgue / Repkit

War Paint

The aggressive Repkit. On use, grants +30% Fire Rate/Speed for 15s. Taking damage reduces its cooldown.

Drop: Saddleback (Old Scrape, Terminus Range)

Manufacturer Guide (New to BL4)

  • Daedalus: Versatile weapons that can utilize multiple ammo types.
  • Order: Precise weapons that can "charge" extra rounds into a single high-damage shot.
  • Ripper: Fully automatic weapons with a wind-up time but extreme fire rates once at max speed.
